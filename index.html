About Me

I am a postdoc researcher at Microsoft Research, New York City Lab. I will join the College of Information Sciences and Technology at Penn State University as an Assistant Professor starting from August 2021. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Virginia under the supervision of Dr. Hongning Wang. I have broad interests in machine learning, with a special focus on methods that can help with online decision-making with uncertainties, such as multi-armed bandits and, more generally, reinforcement learning.

I am looking for motivated students (PhD students starting in Fall 2021 or later). If you are interested, please feel free to send me an email with your CV and submit your PhD admission application through IST Penn State
